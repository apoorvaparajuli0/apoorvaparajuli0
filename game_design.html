Killer Karens!

Killer Karens" is the very first video game level I ever worked on. I created it over the course of a college semester. The game was meant as a group project but everyone in the group was supposed to have their own level. At first I had a whitebox level with no mechanics, no UI, nothing remotely resembling a real video game. But, from the first week I had completely free, which was just a few weeks before the end of the semester, I went through every youtube tutorial I could find, created an insane amount of blueprints that I couldn't even keep track of, and implemented all the mechanics that seemed appropriate for the type of game that my group was going for.
